About Cogni Shield

Our Bank Fraud Detection system is designed to protect financial transactions from fraudulent activities. In the fast-paced world of digital payments, safeguarding sensitive information is critical, and this platform offers a reliable way to do just that.

It helps businesses, financial institutions, and individuals analyze large sets of transaction data quickly and accurately using predefined logic-based checks.

Our goal is to provide an early warning system that prevents financial losses and ensures safer transactions.This project aims to build trust in digital banking by delivering accurate, real-time insights that strengthen financial security and prevent losses before they occur.
